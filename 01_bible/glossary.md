## Powers / Abilities / Systems

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| Plasma | Energy source extracted from Terra. Powers weapons, vehicles, and technology. Is also the fundamental cosmic force — "the beginning and end, the creator, the destroyer, the universe." A massive ball of Plasma is the coming catastrophe. |
| Plasma Manipulation | The ability to control Plasma-infused objects. Required to lift Plasma weapons. Learned through training or innate talent. |
| Sword Art | Combat techniques channeled through Plasma weapons. Includes ranged energy beams, elemental slashes, and special moves. |
| Iaijutsu | Lightning-fast sword draw technique. Master's signature style. Spyke learned it and can use it with Master's katana. Grants extreme speed for a single strike. |
| Elemental Sword | Combined technique — a mage imbues a swordsman's blade with elemental properties (fire, water, ice, thunder). Requires two people. |
| Plasma Gloves | Leather gloves that enable elemental magic by manipulating Plasma and atmospheric elements. Draster wears a pair. Require training to use. |
| Blood of the Ancients | Inherited trait from the civilization of Gaia. Grants: hearing shrine voices, time-freeze perception, visions, communication with ancients. Comes with: eye bleeding, debilitating headaches, demon entity attempting possession. |
| Time-Freeze | Power of the ancients. Freezes all time except the user. Morkain uses it freely. Spyke can perceive it but moves in slow motion within it. Others are completely frozen and unaware. |
| Adrenaline Mode | Spyke's rage-triggered power boost. Eyes turn red, dramatically increased speed and power. Not consciously controlled. |
| Rage Mode | Tutorial ability from Chapter 1. Spyke enters rage state — only way to damage Hood/Morkain in early encounter. |
| Demon Entity | Unknown force inside Spyke connected to blood of the ancients. Manifests as green/purple transformation of eye and arm. Attempts to take control of Spyke's body, especially near crystals and shrines. Says "I'm in control." |
| Stagger | Combat mechanic — enemies must be staggered before taking significant damage. Exploit weaknesses to trigger. |
| Flash of Light | Spyke's advanced katana technique. Splits obstacles and enables short-range teleportation. Learned after Master's dream visit. |
| Combined Spells | Technique where two mages merge elemental spells (e.g., wind + water for safe descent). Requires practice and correct ratios. |

## General Terms

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| Terra | Alien planet that entered the Milky Way solar system. Has blue grass, pink sky, blue-leafed trees, heavy air. Currently paired with Earth, absorbing its lifeforce. |
| Gaia | Terra's previous paired planet. Consumed by Terra. Home of the ancient civilization whose blood Spyke carries. Humans originated from Gaia. |
| Tetra | Original unified planet before Plasma split it into Terra and Gaia. |
| The Catastrophe | A massive ball of Plasma energy heading toward Terra. When it hits, it will devastate Terra and cause Terra to consume Earth's lifeforce. The ancients failed to stop it. Cycle has repeated before (Gaia, Titan). |
| Guardians of Terra | Enormous, unstoppable beasts/monsters that can be summoned using the gnome artefact. |
| Lost Souls | What monsters truly are — the souls of the dead in different form. Can be manufactured using black crystals and corpses/blood. |
| Dukedom | One of 5 territorial divisions of Terra, each originally ruled by an Imperial leader, now controlled by Morkain's appointed Dukes. |
| Imperials | Former government of Terra, led by Supreme Governor Jairek. Overthrown by Morkain. |
| Formican | Adjective referring to the Formican Army and its operations. |
| S-Rank Bounty | Highest difficulty bounty mission. Requires multiple seniors. Master took Spyke and Seymour on one to Goldhawk. |
| Senior | Higher-ranked orphan at Master's dojo. Given leadership responsibilities and allowed on bounty missions. |
| Gil | Currency used on Terra. |

## Naming Conventions

- **Earth locations** use real London place names (Kings Cross, Westminster, Canary Wharf, Goldhawk, Perivale, Welldon Bay).
- **Terra locations** use fictional English-style names (Gunners Green, Stoke Nier, Calderio, Hemington, Knadville, Thicklewig, Sherryville, Bazzleworth).
- **Character names** are original/fictional. The Tinwall surname is shared by Spyke, Seymour, and their father.
- **Dukedoms** are named after their Duke (Hart, Carnaby, Sickleworth, Rainvale) except Bazzleworth.
- **Shrines** are named after zodiac signs (Sagittarius, Cancer, Leo, Gemini + 8 more TBD).
